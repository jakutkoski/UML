Purpose:

The purpose of this application is to help test the patient’s hearing ability.

Requirements:

  • Audiometer
  • Sentences recording (CD, usb, etc.)
  • Internet connected device

Steps:

  1. Start by preparing the audiometer and the patient.
  2. Change the “Speech Level” on the Input Panel to match the audiometer.
  3. Adjust the audiometer’s masker level to match what is shown in the application.
  4. Play sound.
  5. Record the patient’s response in “n of m correct" format where "m" is the number of words presented and "n" is the number of words the patient heard correctly.
  6. Press “Enter”.
  7. Repeat steps 4, 5, and 6 until the test is finished.

Note

  • The Graph Panel will be updated on each Iteration.
  • If the Graph Panel fails to update, you may be using an outdated browser. Please use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
  • You may only change the “Speech Level” once, before the first iteration.
  • Press the "Enter" or "Return" key on your keyboard to present the next masker level.
